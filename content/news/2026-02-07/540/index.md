## How to zoom in or zoom out with one hand in Organic Maps


1. Tap quickly twice anywhere on the map, but don't lift up a finger after the 2nd tap.
2. Move your finger up or down to zoom out or zoom in.

Long Tap gesture: shows or hides the interface.
