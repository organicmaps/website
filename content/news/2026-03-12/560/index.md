The March Organic Maps update took more time due to hotfixes and Google Play review. The [Accrescent][accrescent] update is still not ready due to the Accrescent app store issues. Get the update at <https://get.omaps.org> or on the [App Store][appstore], [Google Play][googleplay], [Huawei AppGallery][appgallery], [Obtainium][obtainium], [Accrescent][accrescent], and [F-Droid][fdroid].

P.S. Contact us by email at <sdk@organicmaps.app> if you are interested in iOS/Android/Linux/MacOS/Windows SDK with offline maps that you can reuse in your projects.

P.P.S. Did you know about the API you can already use in your app if Organic Maps app is also installed? Check it here: <https://omaps.app/api>

### Release notes
* OSM map data as of March 10
* Wikipedia data as of March 1
* More detailed elevation isolines for Chinese provinces (ZiyiGaoCN)
* Added support for conditional speed limits (Abdelhadi Wael, Viktor Govako)
* Added leather shops and shops selling religious goods (Viktor Govako)
* Added rows of trees (Viktor Govako)
* Added public transport stop platforms without nearby bus stops (Viktor Govako)
* Split Tanzania into smaller regions (Emile Villette, Viktor Govako)
* Fixed country downloading progress calculation (Kiryl Kaveryn)
* Fixed several crashes, improved stability and performance (Alexander Borsuk, Viktor Govako)

### iOS
* Added recently used categories history in the Editor (Rahul Kolluru)
* Improved swipe-up gesture on the route planning screen (Kiryl Kaveryn)
* Fixed ruler position for CarPlay (Kiryl Kaveryn)

### Android
* Improved display of business opening hours (Anton P)
* Added gray icon for hidden lists (Alexander Borsuk)

### Translations
* Improved country descriptions (alnzrv)
* Applied Romanian translation fixes by Eugen Dedu (Alexander Borsuk)
* Updated country names (Viktor Govako)
* Updated translations from Weblate contributors (Viktor Govako)
* Fixed Ukrainian translation (Nnifria)
